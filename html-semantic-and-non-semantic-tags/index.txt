1b:T1c19,<h2>Semantic and Non-semantic tags</h2>
<p>Do you know?
<em>Every website you visit has one similarity.</em></p>
<p>Layout, they have layout.</p>
<p>For the entire website what data should be visible on the screen.</p>
<p><strong>Should it contain</strong>? 🤔
A Header, A Hero and A Footer...</p>
<p>Gottcha: <em><code>The layoutting technique comes into existence.</code></em></p>
<h3>What is Layout?</h3>
<p>In simple words layout is the organized HTML elements to build any website.</p>

<p>You may create layout using semantic or non-semantic tags.</p>
